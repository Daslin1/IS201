Life After College

What do I want to do with an Accounting Degree?

Option 1

Ideally, I want to find a job at one of the Big 4 Accounting firms and work my way up the ladder. My dream is to become a partner or at least achieve a high level position.

Option 2

If that doesn't work out for some reason, I would probably apply for an entry-level position at a small business in the area. I may bounce around a little bit, but at least I would be developing my skills.

Option 3

Working for the government is always a stable choice, so I may consider working for the IRS and doing either audits or tax accounting.

Experiences

What kind of experience do I currently have with Accounting?

  • 2021-2022

    Took Accounting Pre-Reqs

    At Brigham Young University I took the Accounting 200 and Accounting 310 courses. These have been the basics for my accounting knowledge.

  • -> Summertime

    My business understanding grew

    I have done door to door sales each summer and I have a better understanding of accounts payable and receivable as a result. I know what it takes to get a business rolling, and I admire the role of the sales team of any company.

  • 2023

    IS 110 & 201

    After taking two information systems and learning tough material, I know the potential that lies in microsoft excel and data analysis. I have a better understanding of what a programmer can do to help in a business.

  • June 2023

    Application

    Although I don't have a ton of accounting experience, I am applying to the program to learn more and become what I have set out to be. I will absorb as much information as I can.
